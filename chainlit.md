# Meridian Electronics — Customer Support

I'm Meridian's customer support assistant. I can help you with:

- **Browse the catalogue** — *"Show me your monitors"*, *"What keyboards do you have?"*, *"List networking gear"*
- **Product details** — *"Tell me about MON-0054"*
- **Verify your account** — to act on your orders, I'll ask for your email and 4-digit PIN
- **Order history** — *"What orders do I have?"*, *"What's the status of order &lt;id&gt;?"*
- **Place a new order** — once verified, *"Order 1 of MON-0054 and 2 of KEY-0010"*

I can't help with returns, refunds, shipping ETAs, account changes, brewing tutorials, weather, or anything outside Meridian's catalogue and orders. If you ask for something out of scope I'll tell you what I can do instead.

The agent reaches a Model Context Protocol (MCP) server for every catalogue and order operation — the MCP server is the source of truth, and tool selection is dynamic.
